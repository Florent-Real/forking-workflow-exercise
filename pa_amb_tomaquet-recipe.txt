Ingredients:
3 baguette
1 garlic
5 tomatoes
1L olive oil
500 g of Iberic ham cut into slices



Instructions:
* Cut every baguette into three equal parts.
* Open each of them into onto two parts (cut along the c_inf symmetry axis)
* Toast each part
* rub the garlic on the toasted bread
* rub the tomato on the garlic-ed toasted bread
* pour a small amount of olive oil
* pace a couple of ham sliced on top.
* Eat&Enjoy
